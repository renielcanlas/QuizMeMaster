Help Center
FAQs
  • Can I use this offline?

    For now, offline capability is not supported, as this is hosted as a web-app. Also, the question database is stored online.

  • Can I my phone to try this?

    Yes you can, this web-app is fully mobile-friendly

  • How to answer questions?

    For Multiple Choices or True/False Questions, just tap your answer and then tap on [Submit] button. Please take note that the chosen answer will have a green background.

  • Can I skip a question?

    Yes you can, but of course, it will be marked as wrong answer

  • Can I view the correct answer?

    Yes, after every answer submission, an alert box will validate your answer, if it is wrong, it will show you the correct answer.

  • How the questions are arranged?

    All the questions are in random order, and for the choices, they are also in random order, so there may be some choices that says "All of the above" but it is placed on the top of the list, consider that as "All of the other answers" :P

  • What are the question types?

    Most of the questions are in Multiple-Choices format, but there are also True/False and Enumeration. Identification or Fill-in-the-blank is also supported, but there are no questions yet.

  • How to answer enumeration type questions?

    You must click the [Add Answer(s)] button and type your answer on the prompt box then choose [OK]

  • Is it case-sensitive?

    No, and also, it is not space-sensitive, this is to minimize unwanted errors

  • Should you provide your answer in order?

    No, you can have your answer in any order

  • What if I mispelled the answer?

    Answer validation will be in substring rule. If the correct item is "Policy Owner", answers like "Policy Owners" will be marked as Correct. Some rules are also applied on dashes, acronyms, etc.

  • What is the scoring system?

    Every correct answer is 1 point, if you finished all the questions, you will receive 10 bonus points
